Summary

My background is in transportation economics with a focus on air travel demand modeling. I am interested in planning, environmental and public policy issues that affect airports and the broader aviation industry. My specialties include airline economics, urban economics, applied econometrics, and data science. As an airport consultant, I assess air travel demand and develop forecasts of aviation activity at airports. The forecasts I develop are key inputs to airport master plans and financial feasibility studies supporting the issuance of bonds to finance airport capital projects, including special facilities like consolidated rental car facilities, parking structures, and terminals. I also prepare airport economic impact studies and benefit-cost analyses for various airport improvement programs.
