Hourly Distribution of Departing/Landing Seats and Arrival Delays at US Airports (US Carrier Domestic Operations, 2013)

Mapping Aircraft Age, Seats and Airport Delays (US Carrier Domestic Operations, 2013)

Data Sources:
  • Airline Delays: Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) On-time Performance Database
  • Aircraft Information: FAA Aircraft Registry

    • Data Notes:
  • Tail(N) numbers reported in the BTS On-time performance data for 2013 were first cleaned. Using a tail number crosswalk, the flight-level data were then merged with the FAA's aircraft inventory, which includes aircraft-specific information on registered owners, manufacturers, age, seats, and engine types. Tail number errors were fixed using information from Planespotters.com, Rzjets.net, Flightstats.com, and Dfwtower.com.
